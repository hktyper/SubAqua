Club Members

Team Solent Sub Aqua or SUCS is the award winning scuba club of Southampton Solent University and is also one of the largest clubs on campus being just over 90 strong.

The club is primarily for students & alumni from Solent University, Warsash Academy and Southampton University, but we also take on Instructors and a limited number of non-students.

As a member of the British Sub-Aqua Club (BSAC), you know you in great hands with Team Solent Sub-Aqua Club.

Members meet in room HCO31 (main campus) every Tuesday from 6.30pm. Additional pool/classroom sessions take place on Thursday, Friday & Saturday, with open water trips on Wednesday, Saturday & Sunday.


Primary training sites are Andark Lake, Vobster Quay & Wraysbury, with Shore diving at Selsey, Swanage and Portland. Hardboat diving takes place at Langstone, Lymington, Poole, Swanage, Weymouth & Plymouth.


During 2014/15, the club members worked hard and were awarded a total of 56 International diving certifications, which worked out at a success rate of 93% of those actively engaged in training. In addition 3 students became full diving Instructors.


Club Members also have the opportunity to travel and take part in dive holidays and events overseas, such as Lanzarote, the Red Sea and Belgium.

Activities

Team Solent offer a variety training activities for students. The following are examples of the main training options provided.


Ocean Diver

Ocean Diver training gets you started in the safety of a swimming pool with a controlled introduction to open water in, safe manner.

Sport Diver

Sports Diver training builds on the Ocean Diver training course, gaining open water diving skills and experience.

Dive Leader

Dive Leader training you prepares you for dive leadership, planning, managing rescue situations and leads to Open Water Instructor training

Advanced Diver

Advanced Diver training further extends skills in dive planning and supervision, rescue management, seamanship and leadership qualities.

Divemaster

Divemaster training brings you up to a high level of experience with all aspects of sports and ocean diving activities.

Nitrox

Nitrox training provides in-depth information of the safe use of gas mixtures during dive sessions and activities and the types of calculations used.

Accelerated Decompression

Accelerated Decompression training builds on the Nitrox training and focuses on techniques to improve the efficiency decompression stops.

Sports Mixed Gas (Trimix)

Sports Mixed Gas training covers the pro’s and con’s of using gas mixes and the prevention of nitrogen narcosis.

Other Options

Other options may be available on occasions, for example fast-track courses. Please enquire for further details.
